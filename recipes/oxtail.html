Oxtail

Description

Jamaican Style Oxtail Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds oxtails
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • teaspoon allspice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion peeled and chopped
  • 4-5 cloves garlic minced
  • 3-4 large carrots peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
  • 2 bell peppers seeded and chopped into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper habanero
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves or 2 tsp dried
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 16 ounces canned butter beans drained
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ½ cup chopped scallions
  • alt and pepper

Steps

  1. Place the oxtails in a large baking dish.
  2. Pour in the Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, 1 teaspoon of black pepper, and 1 teaspoon of allspice.
  3. Toss well to coat the ox tails in the seasonings.
  4. Place a large 6-8 quart saucepot over medium heat.
  5. Add the olive oil to the pot.
  6. Once hot, place half the oxtails in the pot and brown for 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  7. Move the oxtails around the pan so the sugar doesn’t burn on the bottom.
  8. Remove the ox tails and repeat with the remaining second batch.
  9. Add the onions and garlic to the pot.
  10. Sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  11. Top them with the chopped carrots, bell peppers, one whole scotch bonnet pepper, bay leaves, thyme, ketchup, and apple cider vinegar.
  12. Stir the beans into the stew. Simmer another 5-10 minutes.
  13. Sprinkle of scallions, and serve as-is or with a side of rice.
